Exactly Protocol intends to join forces with Uphold to scale and accelerate the adoption of the Exa services. This proposed initiative1, formalized and approved by governance under EXAIP-21 , can open new opportunities for the Exactly ecosystem. By collaborating with Uphold — the infrastructure provider for on-chain finance, serving millions of users across 140+ countries — Exactly is reinforcing its lending market, expanding liquidity, and simplifying access to decentralized finance.
